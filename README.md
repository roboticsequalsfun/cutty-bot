# Cutty-bot (2.0!)

This marvel of engineering ~cuts bananas~ slices various cylindrical delicacies!

It was designed by a younger me who thought the household needed a banana-cutting robot (I was obsessed with useless machines).

And boy… was it bad at it.

It takes around 30 seconds to 1 minute to cut a banana in half, and it can barely grip anything shorter than a banana.

Nevertheless, it’s kind of cool. Good luck trying to replicate this though — LEGO Mindstorms is discontinued, and the app is no longer easily downloadable.

I’m putting this up anyway as a small relic of the internet. Not like anyone is going to memorialize it.

---

# How to use

It’s simple. Okay, that’s a lie. Here are the steps (don’t cry if you get stuck — I did too):

1. You need LEGO Mindstorms, the app, and Studio 2.0.
2. Assemble it using the included instructions (they’re not great).
3. Connect the battery (not your phone).
4. Upload the `.ims` file and insert the battery again (good luck).
5. Try to get it to actually cut a banana.
6. Enjoy your weird bot that mostly just struggles with bananas.
